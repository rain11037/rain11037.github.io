General information to note:

  • Our products may contain common allergens such as nuts, eggs and gluten.
  • As our products are all baked in the same kitchen, we cannot prevent cross-contamination of such allergens.
  • Specific allergens will be listed on our products!

Can I order a flavour from a previous menu?

Sadly, limited flavours can only purchased while they are on our menu. We only sell flavours that are on our current menu, but with enough demand, perhaps some flavours may have a re-run...

Can I order online?

At the moment, we do not have an online ordering service. However, we are working on providing this service, and you can call +65 9788 5608 to place an order. Do note that an extra fee is charged for delivery, so you are advised to come and pick up your treats so they do not get damaged in transit!
